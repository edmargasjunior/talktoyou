Privacy Policy

The TalkToYou application values privacy, safety and user autonomy. This policy explains, in simple language, how information used in the application is handled.

Summary: TalkToYou does not collect, send or share personal data, images, audio recordings or cards with external servers. Information remains stored on the user's own device.

1. Information stored by the app

TalkToYou may store locally on the device:

  • cards created by the user;
  • card and folder names;
  • images chosen or captured by the user;
  • audio recordings created by the user;
  • board settings, such as card size and selected voice;
  • language preference;
  • alarm times configured for cards;
  • layout and accessibility preferences.

2. Where is the data stored?

All data is stored locally on the device using browser or app storage resources, such as IndexedDB and local storage.

TalkToYou does not maintain its own external server to receive, process or store this data.

3. Personal data collection

TalkToYou does not intentionally collect personal data such as name, address, phone number, location, documents, contacts or health records.

Although the app does not request personal data, users may voluntarily create cards containing personal information, photos of people or recorded voices. These contents remain stored locally on the device unless the user exports or shares a backup file.

4. Images and audio recordings

Images and audio recordings added to cards remain on the user's device. They are not automatically sent to the internet, cloud services, external databases or third parties.

The app may request camera, image or microphone permission only when the user chooses to add a photo or record custom audio.

5. Backup and import

The user may export a backup file containing the communication board. This file may include cards, names, images and recorded audio.

The responsibility for storing, protecting or sharing this backup file belongs to the user, family member, caregiver or responsible professional.

Backup files may contain sensitive personal content, such as photos, voices, family routines or personal vocabulary. Store backups in a safe place and avoid sharing them with unknown people.

6. Device permissions

TalkToYou may request permission to:

  • access images or camera when the user wants to add a photo to a card;
  • access the microphone when the user wants to record custom audio;
  • use local browser/app storage to save information on the device.

These permissions are used only for features intentionally activated by the user.

7. Sharing with third parties

TalkToYou does not share user information with third parties.

The app does not automatically send cards, images, audio recordings or communication board content to developers, companies, advertisers, analytics platforms or external databases.

8. Use by children and vulnerable users

TalkToYou is designed as a support tool for communication. When used by children or people who require assistance, use should be supervised by parents, guardians, caregivers, teachers, therapists or responsible professionals.

9. Purpose and limitations of the application

TalkToYou is an alternative and augmentative communication support tool. It does not replace evaluation, diagnosis, treatment, therapy or professional guidance from speech-language pathologists, occupational therapists, psychologists, physicians or other qualified professionals.

10. Language and personalized content

The app may offer interface language options, such as Portuguese, English and Spanish.

Cards created by the user are not automatically translated. This protects names, family routines, therapy phrases, cultural expressions and individualized communication choices.

11. Deleting data

Data may be removed by:

  • deleting cards inside the app;
  • using the local data clearing option in the app;
  • clearing browser/app data through device settings;
  • uninstalling the app from the device.
Before deleting local data, users are encouraged to export a backup if they want to preserve their communication board.

12. Offline use and cache

TalkToYou may support offline use through local storage and, in future versions, through Service Worker caching.

Offline resources are intended to improve availability and do not imply that personal content is sent to external servers.

13. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect improvements in the app, changes in distribution platforms, accessibility updates or legal requirements.

14. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer responsible for the application.
